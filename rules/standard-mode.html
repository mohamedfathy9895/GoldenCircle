Standard Mode: The Complete Experience

Standard Mode offers the full Golden Circle: Purpose to Impact experience, presenting a balanced challenge for players familiar with strategy board games. This mode fully implements Simon Sinek's "Start with Why" philosophy while providing engaging gameplay mechanics.

Note: This is the default game mode recommended for most player groups. If you're new to the game, consider trying Beginner Mode first for an introduction to the core concepts.

WHY Construction & Evolution

Building Your WHY

Your organization's purpose (WHY) is constructed from three component types:

  1. Core Value: What your organization fundamentally believes in (e.g., Innovation, Integrity, Impact)
  2. Beneficiary: Who benefits from your purpose (e.g., Customers, Society, Future Generations)
  3. Method: How you deliver on your purpose (e.g., Through Technology, Through Service, Through Leadership)

Example WHY Statement: "We believe in [INNOVATION] for [FUTURE GENERATIONS] through [TECHNOLOGY]"

WHY Component Levels

Each WHY component can evolve through three levels of increasing power and specificity:

  • Level 1: Basic component with a simple ability
  • Level 2: Enhanced component with stronger abilities and synergy options
  • Level 3: Fully evolved component with powerful abilities, scoring benefits, and crisis protection

Upgrading WHY Components:

  • Spend 3 Action Points to begin the upgrade process for a WHY component
  • Place an upgrade token on the component
  • When you reach a Milestone space, you may complete one pending upgrade
  • You cannot have more than one pending upgrade at a time

WHY Alignment

Your Alignment score (0-10) measures how consistently your actions support your stated purpose:

  • High Alignment (8-10): Unlocks WHY abilities, provides crisis protection, and scoring bonuses
  • Moderate Alignment (5-7): Standard gameplay
  • Low Alignment (2-4): Reduced effectiveness, vulnerability to certain crises
  • Misalignment (0-1): Severe penalties, higher costs, crisis vulnerability

Alignment increases when you take actions that support your WHY and decreases when you contradict it.

Game Setup (Standard Mode)

  1. Select Industry: Each player chooses one of the 6 Industry Focus cards
  2. Draw WHY Components:
    • Draw 2 Core Value cards, select 1
    • Draw 2 Beneficiary cards, select 1
    • Draw 2 Method cards, select 1
    (Combine these to form your unique WHY statement)
  3. Draw Starting Cards:
    • 3 HOW Strategy cards, keep 2
    • 3 WHAT Product/Service cards, keep 2
  4. Collect Resources: 5 Staff, 5 Capital, 5 Brand, 5 Innovation Points
  5. Set Trackers: 5 Market Share, 5 Public Trust, 5 Innovation, 5 Alignment
  6. Place Pawns: All players start at the "Inspiration Point" space

Turn Sequence

1
Roll & Move: Roll the dice and move your pawn that many spaces
2
Space Action: Resolve the space you landed on
  • WHY Space: Draw a WHY component card
  • HOW Space: Draw a HOW strategy card
  • WHAT Space: Draw a WHAT product/service card
  • Crisis Space: Draw and resolve a Crisis card
  • Market Space: Draw a Market Opportunity card
  • Milestone Space: Complete a pending WHY upgrade, gain bonus
3
Action Phase: Spend 3-5 Action Points on various activities:
Action AP Cost Description
Play HOW Card 1 Implement a HOW Strategy card from your hand (pay any resource costs)
Play WHAT Card 2 Develop a WHAT Product/Service from your hand (pay any resource costs)
Begin WHY Upgrade 3 Start upgrading a WHY component (completed at Milestone)
Partner Action 2 Form partnership, collaborate on WHAT card, or share resources
Crisis Preparation 1 Gain Crisis Protection for a specific crisis type
Resource Investment 1 Gain +2 resources of your choice (any distribution)
Market Research 1 Look at top 3 cards of any deck, keep 1, return others
4
Alignment Check: Verify if your actions align with your WHY
  • +1 Alignment for each action that strongly supports your WHY
  • No change for neutral actions
  • -1 Alignment for each action that contradicts your WHY
5
Update Trackers: Adjust progress tracks and resource tokens
  • Market Share: Commercial success and reach
  • Public Trust: Stakeholder confidence and reputation
  • Innovation: Creative and adaptive capabilities
  • Resources: Staff, Capital, Brand, Innovation Points

Crisis Management

When you land on a Crisis space, draw a Crisis card and follow these steps:

Crisis Resolution

  1. Check Protection: Determine if you have automatic protection:
    • From a WHY component ability
    • From a HOW strategy card
    • From prior Crisis Preparation action
  2. Pay Mitigation Cost: If not protected, you may pay the mitigation cost shown on the card
  3. Choose Resolution Path: Crisis cards offer 2+ resolution options with different costs and outcomes
  4. Alignment Impact: Some crisis resolutions affect your Alignment score

Important: If you cannot or choose not to mitigate a crisis, you must take the full penalty AND choose a resolution path.

Crisis Categories

  • Market Disruptions: Competition, technology shifts, industry changes
  • Public Relations: Scandals, social media backlash, brand challenges
  • Resource Challenges: Talent exodus, supply chain issues, funding
  • Leadership Tests: Ethical dilemmas, purpose conflicts, vision challenges
  • Global Events: Economic changes, regulations, societal shifts

Example Crisis Card: "Data Privacy Scandal" (PR Crisis)

Impact: Lose 3 Public Trust unless you have a Trust-focused HOW strategy OR pay 4 Innovation Points.

Resolution Options:

  1. Public apology (regain 1 Trust but lose 1 Market Share)
  2. Deny responsibility (keep Market Share but risk -2 additional Trust)

Strategic Partnerships

Partnerships allow players to collaborate for mutual benefit:

Forming Partnerships

  • Spend 2 Action Points to propose a partnership to another player
  • The other player must accept or decline
  • Both players record the partnership on their player boards

Partnership Benefits

  • Collaborative WHAT Cards: Partner on product development for shared benefits
  • Resource Sharing: Once per round, partners can trade resources at a 1:1 ratio
  • Crisis Support: Partners can contribute to crisis mitigation costs
  • Synergy Bonuses: Certain HOW and WHAT cards grant additional benefits when in partnerships

Partnership Management

  • Partnerships last until dissolved by either partner (costs 1 Action Point)
  • Breaking a partnership without mutual consent causes -2 Public Trust
  • Maximum of 2 active partnerships per player

Winning the Game

The game ends when one of these conditions is met:

  • A player reaches 25+ points on the Market Share track
  • A player completes one full circuit of the board and reaches the Golden Circle Center
  • The Market Revolution Event Card is drawn (time-based end trigger)

Victory Conditions

Players can win through different paths:

Purpose Fulfillment

Reach the Golden Circle Center after completing one full circuit with 9+ Alignment

Market Dominance

Achieve 25+ points on the Market Share track

Triple Crown

Achieve at least 15 points on all three tracks (Market Share, Public Trust, Innovation)

Final Scoring

If no player achieves one of the victory conditions above, the winner is determined by final score:

  1. Sum of all three progress tracks (Market Share + Public Trust + Innovation)
  2. +1 point for each Level 2 WHY component
  3. +2 points for each Level 3 WHY component
  4. +3 points if Alignment is 9 or 10
  5. +1 point for each crisis successfully mitigated

Example Final Score: Alex has 16 Market Share + 14 Public Trust + 12 Innovation = 42 base points

Bonuses: +2 for Level 2 WHY components, +3 for High Alignment, +2 for crises mitigated = 7 bonus points

Final Score: 49 points

Advanced Strategies (Optional)

These optional rules add strategic depth for players familiar with Standard Mode:

WHY Pivoting

Once per game, at a Milestone space, you may completely pivot one WHY component:

  • Discard your current component and select a new one from your hand or the deck
  • The new component enters at Level 1, regardless of the prior component's level
  • Gain +1 Alignment if the new component better fits your strategy
  • Gain a temporary -1 Action Point penalty for 1 round as you realign

Market Influencing

Spend Innovation Points to influence the market:

  • 3 Innovation Points: Look at the top 3 Crisis cards, rearrange them
  • 2 Innovation Points: Modify a space effect (one time)
  • 4 Innovation Points: Create a temporary market condition that benefits one of your WHAT cards

Purpose Storytelling

Once per game, deliver a "Purpose Pitch" to all players:

  • Explain how your WHY, HOW, and WHAT work together
  • Other players vote on how compelling your narrative is
  • Gain 1-3 Public Trust based on votes

Ready for More Challenge?

Once you've mastered Standard Mode, consider trying Expert CEO Mode, which includes:

  • Dynamic market conditions that change throughout gameplay
  • Forced WHY pivot events that test adaptability
  • Cascading crisis chains with complex resolution paths
  • Industry disruption events that can completely change the game board
  • Purpose authenticity audits with potential for alignment penalties
  • Complex scoring with multiple paths to victory

Recommendation: Play at least 2-3 games in Standard Mode before attempting Expert CEO Mode.
