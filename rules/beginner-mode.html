Beginner Mode: Learning the Golden Circle

Beginner Mode is designed for first-time players to learn the core mechanics of Golden Circle while streamlining gameplay and reducing complexity. This mode emphasizes understanding Simon Sinek's "Start with Why" philosophy through accessible gameplay.

Note: In Beginner Mode, all cards and game pieces have special visual indicators (blue dots) to help identify the recommended options for new players.

Beginner Mode Modifications

1. Simplified WHY Construction

Instead of building a custom WHY from individual components:

  • Players choose from 6 pre-built WHY statements (each with all three components already combined)
  • All WHY statements start at Level 1 with clear abilities
  • WHY upgrades are automatic at milestone spaces (no decisions required)

Example Pre-built WHY: "We believe in INNOVATION for FUTURE GENERATIONS through TECHNOLOGY"

Ability: Gain +1 Innovation Point when playing Technology-based WHAT cards; Technology HOW cards cost 1 less.

2. Limited Crisis Challenges

Crisis management is simplified:

  • Only basic crisis cards are included (advanced and cascading crises removed)
  • Crisis cards always offer clear mitigation options
  • Players receive 1 "Crisis Skip" token that can be used once per game
  • Reduced crisis penalties (maximum -2 on any track)

3. Resource Bonuses

More generous resource management:

  • Start with 7 of each resource instead of 5
  • Gain +1 of each resource automatically each turn
  • No resource upkeep costs
  • Passing a milestone grants +3 of each resource

4. Simplified Scoring

Focus on learning the board movement and card plays:

  • Primary focus on Market Share track (simplified victory condition)
  • Automatic +1 Alignment when playing cards that match your WHY (no complex alignment checks)
  • 20 Market Share needed for victory (instead of 25)
  • Reduced board path (30 spaces instead of 40)

5. Guided Card Choices

Cards include beginner-friendly features:

  • Recommended combinations marked with "beginner" icons
  • Simplified card effects (no conditional or delayed benefits)
  • Hand cards are always visible to all players for learning
  • Recommended card text suggesting optimal plays

6. Action Simplification

Streamlined turn structure:

  • Fixed 3 Action Points per turn (no saving or varying amounts)
  • Simple Action Point costs (all actions cost 1 AP)
  • No complex partnerships or alliances
  • Simple market spaces with clear benefits

Beginner Mode Setup

  1. Select Industry: Each player chooses one of the 6 Industry Focus cards
  2. Choose WHY: Select one of the 6 pre-built WHY statements (try to choose one that fits your Industry)
  3. Draw Starting Cards:
    • 2 HOW Strategy cards (marked with blue beginner dots)
    • 2 WHAT Product cards (marked with blue beginner dots)
  4. Collect Resources: 7 Staff, 7 Capital, 7 Brand, 7 Innovation Points
  5. Set Trackers: 5 Market Share, 5 Public Trust, 5 Innovation, 5 Alignment
  6. Place Pawns: All players start at the "Inspiration Point" space

Beginner Mode Gameplay

Turn Sequence

1
Roll & Move: Roll one die and move your pawn that many spaces
2
Space Action: Resolve the space you landed on (draw a card or follow the simple space instruction)
3
Take Actions: Spend 3 Action Points on any of these options:
  • Play a HOW card (1 AP)
  • Play a WHAT card (1 AP)
  • Gain +2 resources of your choice (1 AP)
  • Draw a card (1 AP)
4
Update Tracks: Adjust all progress tracks based on card effects
5
Resource Phase: Gain +1 of each resource automatically

Milestone Corners

When you reach or pass one of the milestone corners:

  • Your WHY automatically upgrades to the next level
  • Gain +3 of each resource
  • Draw 1 card of your choice

Crisis Cards (Simplified)

If you land on a Crisis space:

  • Draw a Crisis card (blue dot beginner versions only)
  • Choose ONE option to mitigate the crisis:
    1. Pay the resources shown on the card
    2. Use a Crisis Protection ability from one of your cards
    3. Use your one-time Crisis Skip token
  • If you cannot or choose not to mitigate, take the penalty (maximum -2 to any one track)

Learning Tip: In Beginner Mode, you can ask for advice from other players when facing a crisis. The game is designed to be a collaborative learning experience.

Winning in Beginner Mode

The game ends when:

  • A player reaches 20+ Market Share points (Market Dominance victory)
  • OR a player reaches the Golden Circle Center space
  • OR after 10 complete rounds

To determine the winner:

  1. If any player reached 20+ Market Share, they win immediately
  2. If any player reached the Golden Circle Center with 7+ Alignment, they win
  3. Otherwise, the player with the highest combined score across all three tracks wins

Example Score Calculation: Sarah has 15 Market Share + 8 Public Trust + 10 Innovation Points = 33 Total Score

Transitioning to Standard Mode

Once you've played Beginner Mode and understand the basic mechanics, you're ready to try Standard Mode, which offers:

  • Custom WHY construction from individual components
  • Strategic WHY upgrades with meaningful choices
  • Full crisis management with varied resolution options
  • Resource management and strategic decisions
  • Strategic partnerships and alliances
  • More complex card effects and synergies

Recommendation: Play 1-2 games in Beginner Mode before moving to Standard Mode. This ensures you understand the core mechanics and Golden Circle philosophy.
