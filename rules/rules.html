Game Concept

Golden Circle: Purpose to Impact is a strategy board game where players lead fictional companies on a journey to define their "WHY", implement impactful strategies (HOW), and develop meaningful offerings (WHAT). Success comes from alignment with purpose, navigating crises, and mastering business strategy.

Based on Simon Sinek's "Start with Why" philosophy, this game challenges players to build purpose-driven organizations that can thrive in changing market conditions while staying true to their core beliefs.

Game Objective

The goal is to build a purpose-driven organization that achieves success through one of these victory conditions:

Purpose Fulfillment

Reach the Golden Circle Center after completing one full circuit with 90+ Alignment

Market Dominance

Achieve 25+ points on the Market Share track

Triple Crown

Achieve at least 15 points on all three tracks (Market Share, Public Trust, Innovation)

Legacy Score

Have the highest total score after all players complete the board circuit

Core Concepts

The Golden Circle

The core philosophy behind the game is Simon Sinek's Golden Circle:

  • WHY - The purpose, cause or belief that drives your organization
  • HOW - The methods, processes, and approaches you use
  • WHAT - The products, services, and offerings you provide

Organizations that operate from the inside-out (starting with WHY) tend to inspire loyalty, innovation, and resilience.

Alignment

Throughout the game, your decisions will be measured against your stated WHY. Actions that support your purpose increase Alignment; those that contradict it decrease Alignment. High alignment unlocks powerful abilities and protects against crises.

Note: Alignment is not the same as being "good" or "ethical" - it's about consistency with your stated purpose, whatever that may be.

Resources

You'll manage three key resources:

  • Staff - Represents human capital, talent, and skills
  • Capital - Represents financial resources and investments
  • Brand - Represents reputation and market presence

Innovation Points are a special fourth resource that can be spent on R&D or Crisis Mitigation.

Progress Tracks

Your success is measured on three progress tracks:

  • Market Share - Your commercial success and reach
  • Public Trust - Stakeholder confidence and loyalty
  • Innovation - Your organization's creative and adaptive capabilities

Game Flow Overview

1
Roll & Move - Roll dice and move along the Golden Path
2
Resolve Space - Draw cards or handle events based on space type
3
Action Phase - Spend 3-5 Action Points on various activities
4
Alignment Check - Verify if actions align with your WHY
5
Update Trackers - Adjust progress tracks and resource tokens

Example Turn: Sarah rolls a 4 and lands on a WHY Space. She draws a WHY Component card "INNOVATION (Level 1)". She then spends 2 Action Points to play a HOW card "Agile Development" and 2 more Action Points to play a WHAT card "Mobile App Platform". Both cards align with her WHY, so she gains +1 Alignment. She then updates her Market Share (+2) and Innovation (+1) tracks.

Standard Mode

The default game experience with balanced gameplay:

  • Build your WHY from component cards
  • Face a normal mix of crises and opportunities
  • Start with balanced resources
  • All three progress tracks contribute to victory

Recommended for players familiar with strategy board games or who have played Beginner Mode once.
