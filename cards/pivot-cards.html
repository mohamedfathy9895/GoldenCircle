Pivot Opportunity Cards

Pivot cards are special opportunity cards available only to players who are falling behind or facing significant challenges. These cards provide powerful catch-up mechanisms and strategic pivots that can transform your organization's trajectory.

PIVOT - WHY REDEFINITION
PURPOSE EVOLUTION
"Adapting to a Changing World"

Eligibility: Available if your Alignment score is below 6 OR you are in last place for Market Share.

Pivot Effect:

Replace any ONE component of your WHY statement with a new component of the same type from your hand or the draw pile. Your new WHY component enters at the same level as the replaced component.

Bonus Effect:

  • Gain +2 Alignment immediately
  • All players receive notice of your purpose evolution (draw a card)
  • You may discard up to 3 cards from your hand to draw 3 new cards
I
T

Strategic Consideration: This pivot is powerful but may require adjusting your HOW and WHAT cards to maintain alignment with your new purpose direction.

PIVOT - WHY EXPANSION
PURPOSE CLARIFICATION
"Discovering Deeper Meaning"

Eligibility: Available if you have not upgraded any WHY component to Level 2 by Round 3.

Pivot Effect:

Immediately upgrade one of your WHY components to Level 2 without paying the normal cost or action points. Gain a clear narrative about what your organization truly stands for.

Bonus Effect:

  • Gain +1 Public Trust
  • Gain +1 Innovation Point
  • Your next HOW card costs 1 fewer Action Point to play
T

Strategic Consideration: Use this to reinforce your strongest WHY component rather than fixing a weak one. The reinforcement strategy typically yields better long-term results.

PIVOT - HOW TRANSFORMATION
STRATEGIC OVERHAUL
"Reimagining Your Approach"

Eligibility: Available if you have lost Market Share for 2 consecutive rounds OR have faced an unmitigated crisis.

Pivot Effect:

Discard all your current HOW strategy cards. Draw 3 new HOW cards and immediately implement 2 of them for no action point cost.

Bonus Effect:

  • Gain immunity to the next crisis you face
  • Gain +2 Innovation Points
  • Rebuild your team: Gain +2 Staff resources
I
D

Risk Factor: -1 Alignment unless your new HOW strategies clearly support your WHY statement.

PIVOT - HOW ENHANCEMENT
STRATEGY ACCELERATION
"Doubling Down on What Works"

Eligibility: Available if you have no more than 2 HOW cards in play.

Pivot Effect:

Select one of your HOW strategy cards. Double its positive effects for the next 3 rounds. You also gain access to its crisis protection abilities even if you don't meet the normal requirements.

Bonus Effect:

  • Draw 2 additional HOW cards
  • Future HOW cards cost -1 resource to implement for 2 rounds
  • Gain +1 Alignment if the boosted HOW card strongly supports your WHY
C

Strategic Consideration: Most effective when used on HOW cards that give ongoing benefits rather than one-time effects.

PIVOT - WHAT BREAKTHROUGH
DISRUPTIVE INNOVATION
"Game-Changing Solution"

Eligibility: Available if you are trailing in Innovation Points OR have fewer WHAT cards than any other player.

Pivot Effect:

Immediately draw 3 WHAT cards and play one for no resource cost. This WHAT card implementation ignores all prerequisites and resource requirements.

Bonus Effect:

  • Gain +3 Innovation Points
  • The chosen WHAT card gains +1 on all its impact tracks
  • Competitors cannot copy this WHAT card for 2 rounds
I
D

Strategic Consideration: Best used to implement a high-cost, high-impact WHAT card that would normally be out of reach.

PIVOT - RESOURCE INFUSION
EMERGENCY FUNDING
"Belief in Your Potential"

Eligibility: Available if you have fewer total resources than any other player OR have lost resources to a crisis in the last round.

Pivot Effect:

Immediately gain 7 resources of your choice, distributed as you wish among Staff, Capital, Brand, and Innovation Points.

Bonus Effect:

  • Your next crisis card may be discarded without effect
  • Draw 1 Opportunity card
  • Gain +1 Action Point on your next turn
C
I

Strategic Consideration: Distribute resources to address your most critical shortages while ensuring you have enough of each type to play your next planned cards.

PIVOT - MARKET REPOSITIONING
CATEGORY LEADERSHIP
"Redefining Your Market"

Eligibility: Available if you are in last or second-to-last place in Market Share.

Pivot Effect:

Immediately gain +4 Market Share. Additionally, create a new market category where you are the default leader (choose a combination of two symbols, like Innovation + Trust).

Bonus Effect:

  • Any WHAT cards matching your category symbols gain +1 Market Share
  • Gain +2 Public Trust for pioneering this category
  • Other players must spend 2 Innovation Points to enter your category
I
D
T

Duration: Category leader benefits last for 3 rounds

Strategic Consideration: Choose symbols that align with your WHY and your planned future WHAT cards to maximize the lasting benefit.

PIVOT - STRATEGIC ALLIANCE
POWER PARTNERSHIP
"Strength in Collaboration"

Eligibility: Available if you have no current partnerships OR if you are trailing in Public Trust.

Pivot Effect:

Form a powerful alliance with the current market leader. Both players gain +2 Market Share, +1 Public Trust, and +1 Innovation Point immediately.

Bonus Effect:

  • You may use one of your partner's HOW cards as if it were your own
  • Your partner draws a card of their choice
  • You gain +1 Alignment if the partnership aligns with your WHY
C
T

Duration: Partnership lasts for 4 rounds or until either player chooses to end it

Strategic Consideration: This partnership benefits you more than the market leader, but they cannot refuse the offer.

PIVOT - PLATFORM CREATION
ECOSYSTEM LAUNCH
"Building the Foundation"

Eligibility: Available if you have no Platform-type WHAT cards OR your highest scoring track is at least 5 points behind the leader.

Pivot Effect:

Immediately implement a Platform-type WHAT card from your hand or the deck for half the normal resource cost (rounded up). This Platform provides +3 to your weakest progress track.

Bonus Effect:

  • Your next 2 WHAT cards that build on this Platform cost 1 fewer resource each
  • Gain Crisis Protection related to your Platform's focus area
  • Gain +2 Alignment if the Platform strongly supports your WHY
I
C
S

Strategic Consideration: This pivot creates a foundation for future growth. Be ready to build upon it with compatible WHAT cards in subsequent turns.
