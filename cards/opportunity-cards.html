Opportunity Cards

Opportunity cards represent positive market shifts, trends, or events that can boost your organization if you're positioned to capitalize on them. These cards often provide temporary benefits or strategic advantages.

OPPORTUNITY - MARKET TREND
CONSCIOUS CONSUMERISM WAVE
"Values-Based Purchasing Takes Off"

Opportunity Effect:

A significant shift toward ethical consumption is occurring. If your WHY includes "Society" or "Environment," gain +2 Public Trust and +1 Market Share.

Strategic Options:

  • Emphasize your purpose in marketing (spend 1 Brand to gain +1 additional Trust)
  • Upgrade a product to align with this trend (spend 1 Innovation Point to gain +1 additional Market Share)
  • Form partnership with aligned organization (spend 1 Action Point to gain +1 Trust and +1 Market Share)
S
T

Duration: 2 rounds

OPPORTUNITY - MARKET TREND
DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION SURGE
"Tech Adoption Accelerates"

Opportunity Effect:

Organizations are rapidly embracing digital solutions. If you have Technology in your WHY or any Digital WHAT products, gain +2 Market Share and +2 Innovation Points.

Strategic Options:

  • Accelerate innovation pipeline (spend 2 Innovation Points to gain +2 additional Market Share)
  • Launch tech education initiative (spend 1 Staff to gain +2 Public Trust)
  • Acquire a tech startup (spend 3 Capital to gain a random Technology WHAT card)
I
D

Duration: 3 rounds

OPPORTUNITY - INVESTMENT
IMPACT INVESTOR INTEREST
"Purpose-Driven Funding"

Opportunity Effect:

Impact investors are looking for purpose-driven businesses. If your Alignment is 8+, gain +3 Capital resources.

Strategic Options:

  • Scale operations (gain +2 additional Capital but -1 Alignment)
  • Enhance purpose initiatives (gain +1 Capital and +1 Alignment)
  • Decline funding (gain +2 Alignment but no Capital)
C
T

Duration: Immediate effect

OPPORTUNITY - PARTNERSHIP
STRATEGIC ALLIANCE OPENING
"Collaborate for Impact"

Opportunity Effect:

A complementary organization seeks a partnership. Choose another player to form an alliance with. Both players gain +1 Market Share and +1 Public Trust.

Strategic Options:

  • Form a Research Alliance (both gain +2 Innovation Points)
  • Form a Market Alliance (both gain +1 additional Market Share)
  • Form a Trust Alliance (both gain +1 additional Public Trust)
C

Duration: 4 rounds (breaking alliance early causes -2 Trust)
