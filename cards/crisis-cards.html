Crisis Cards

Crisis cards represent unexpected challenges that test how well your business decisions align with your WHY. These cards can affect resources, alignment, and market position.

CRISIS - MARKET DISRUPTION
TECH OBSOLESCENCE THREAT
"Your tech stack is suddenly outdated"

Crisis Impact:

A competitor has launched a superior technology. Lose 2 Market Share and 1 Innovation Point.

Protection Options:

  • Have an Innovation-focused WHY
  • OR Spend 3 Innovation Points
  • OR Have "Continuous Innovation" HOW card

Crisis Resolution: Choose to pivot your technology (spend 2 Capital) OR maintain current tech (Risk -1 Market Share per round for 2 rounds)

Expert Mode: Triggers "Industry Shift" cascade crisis if unmitigated

CRISIS - MARKET DISRUPTION
NEW MARKET ENTRANT
"A disruptive startup appears"

Crisis Impact:

A well-funded startup with a novel approach enters your market. Lose 3 Market Share.

Protection Options:

  • Have a Disruption-focused HOW card
  • OR Spend 2 Innovation Points + 1 Capital
  • OR High Alignment (8+) with Innovation WHY

Crisis Resolution: Acquire the startup (spend 4 Capital) OR compete directly (spend 2 Innovation Points)

Expert Mode: Reduces effect of "Growth Strategy" HOW cards by 50% for 2 rounds if unmitigated

CRISIS - PUBLIC RELATIONS
DATA PRIVACY SCANDAL
"Your data practices are under scrutiny"

Crisis Impact:

A major data breach has occurred. Lose 3 Public Trust points.

Protection Options:

  • Have a Trust-focused HOW strategy
  • OR Pay 4 Innovation Points to mitigate
  • OR Have "Ethical Data Practices" card

Crisis Resolution: Public apology (regain 1 Trust but lose 1 Market Share) OR deny responsibility (keep Market Share but risk -2 additional Trust if failed roll)

Expert Mode: If unmitigated, all Trust gains are halved for 2 rounds

CRISIS - PUBLIC RELATIONS
SOCIAL MEDIA BACKLASH
"Your brand is trending for the wrong reasons"

Crisis Impact:

A viral criticism of your practices is spreading. Lose 3 Public Trust points and 1 Brand resource.

Protection Options:

  • Have INTEGRITY in your WHY components
  • OR Spend 2 Brand resources
  • OR Have "Community Engagement" HOW card

Crisis Resolution: Engage directly (spend 1 Staff, potentially gain 1 Trust) OR wait for it to blow over (50% chance to lose 1 more Trust)

Expert Mode: If at Low Alignment, automatically triggers "Leadership Credibility" crisis

CRISIS - RESOURCE CHALLENGE
TALENT EXODUS
"Key team members are leaving"

Crisis Impact:

Several key employees have resigned. Lose 2 Staff resources and 1 Innovation Point.

Protection Options:

  • Have a HOW card that includes COLLABORATION
  • OR High Alignment score (9+)
  • OR Have "Talent Development" strategy

Crisis Resolution: Invest in culture (spend 2 Capital, gain future protection) OR hire replacements quickly (spend 3 Capital, no lasting effects)

Expert Mode: Next WHAT card costs +1 Staff if unmitigated

CRISIS - LEADERSHIP TEST
ETHICAL DILEMMA
"Your values are being tested"

Crisis Impact:

A major opportunity arises, but taking it would contradict your stated WHY.

Protection Options:

  • None - This crisis tests your commitment to purpose

Crisis Resolution: Stay true to WHY (lose 2 Market Share but gain +2 Alignment) OR compromise (gain 3 Market Share but lose 3 Alignment)

Expert Mode: If you compromise, all future Alignment checks are at -1 penalty

CRISIS - GLOBAL EVENT
ECONOMIC DOWNTURN
"The market contracts suddenly"

Crisis Impact:

A recession hits the economy. All players lose 2 Capital resources and 1 Market Share.

Protection Options:

  • Have "RESILIENCE" strategy card
  • OR Have diversified WHAT portfolio (3+ different types)
  • OR Spend 3 Capital to weather the storm

Crisis Resolution: Cut costs (lose 1 Staff but preserve Capital) OR invest counter-cyclically (spend 2 Capital, gain potential future Market Share bonus)

Expert Mode: All resource costs increase by 1 for 2 rounds if unmitigated

CRISIS - GLOBAL EVENT
REGULATORY CHANGE
"The rules of business have changed"

Crisis Impact:

New regulations affect your industry. Discard 1 WHAT card OR lose 2 Market Share.

Protection Options:

  • Have "Regulatory Affairs" HOW card
  • OR High Trust score (8+)
  • OR Spend 2 Innovation Points to adapt quickly

Crisis Resolution: Lobby against changes (spend 2 Brand + 1 Capital) OR embrace and position as leader (spend 2 Innovation Points, potential Trust gain)

Expert Mode: If unmitigated, one random HOW card becomes ineffective until next milestone
