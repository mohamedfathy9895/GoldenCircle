Game Concept

A strategy board game where players lead fictional companies on a journey to define their "WHY", implement impactful strategies (HOW), and develop meaningful offerings (WHAT). Success comes from alignment with purpose, navigating crises, and mastering business strategy.

Players

2-5 players

Game Time

60-120 minutes

Complexity

Medium

Difficulty Levels

Beginner Mode

Pre-built WHYs, limited crises, guided card choices

Standard Mode

Build full WHY, full deck use, balanced crises

Expert CEO Mode

Dynamic market, forced WHY pivots, crisis chains
